We are a highly talented, ISO 9001:2008 certified design house based in Nairobi. We provide services in architecture, interior design, branded environments. archiviz, and construction project management to a variety of private and public clients. We are registered by the Board of Registration of Architects and Quantity Surveyors (BORAQS). We are also members of the Architectural Association of Kenya (AAK) and the Royal Institute of British Architecture (RIBA).


Our projects are diverse; in terms of type, scale and use. In collaboration with our highly specialized partners, we also offer engineering, quantity surveying, development management and brand development services. Our design team is optimized to address the specific needs and circumstances of each assignment.
To guarantee quality, our project teams research extensively and innovate solutions for every project. Our chief mission is creating extraordinary places of world class quality. Designing places that lift the spirit. We envision to become the number one design house for Africa. To sit comfortably with the best in the world. To have countless satisfied users of the magical places that we strive to create; every clients' dream. To achieve these, we will remain firm, focussed and passionate.
